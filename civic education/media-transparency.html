Media and Transparency in Kenyan Government

The media plays a vital role in keeping the government accountable and ensuring transparency in the decision-making process. By investigating, reporting, and scrutinizing government policies and actions, the media serves as a watchdog, empowering citizens to make informed decisions and hold their leaders responsible.

Media's Role in Government Accountability

The Kenyan media landscape is diverse, with a range of independent print, broadcast, and digital news outlets. These media organizations often investigate government activities, expose corruption, and provide a platform for public discourse on important issues. By holding the government accountable, the media helps ensure that the interests of the people are being served.
